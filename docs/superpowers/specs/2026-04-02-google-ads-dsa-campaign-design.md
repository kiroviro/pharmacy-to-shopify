## Context

- PMax campaign "Feb 2026.3" spent **€1,928** with only **€1,048** Shopify revenue — **0.54x ROAS**
- Root cause: all 7 conversion events were set as Primary, inflating reported conversions to 38,205 (115% conv. rate). Google optimized for page views, not purchases.
- Google Shopping / Merchant Center feed is **not available in Bulgaria** — "No products for any locations" is a platform limitation
- Google partner recommended Dynamic Search Ads as the best approach for Bulgaria
- PMax campaign paused on 2026-04-02

At €10/day with €0.50 max CPC, worst case monthly spend is €310 — **5x less** than PMax was burning.

## Lessons Learned (from PMax failure)

1. **Never set micro-conversions as Primary** — page_view, search, view_item should always be Secondary
2. **Google Shopping / Merchant Center is not available in Bulgaria** — do not depend on Shopping feed
3. **Start with conservative budgets** — €10/day for cold-start, scale only after real purchase conversions flow
4. **Manual bidding caps for new campaigns** — don't let the algorithm run unconstrained until there's enough purchase data
5. **Verify conversion tracking before enabling any campaign** — check Goals → Conversions, confirm only purchase is Primary
6. **Cross-reference Google Ads "conversions" with Shopify orders** — if conversion rate exceeds ~5%, something is misconfigured
